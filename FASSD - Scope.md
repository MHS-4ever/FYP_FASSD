# **Final Scope of Project**

1. ## **AI vs Human Voice Detection**

   Detect whether a full audio recording is natural human speech or AI-generated synthetic speech.

2. ## **Detection of AI-Replaced Voices in Real Recordings**

   Identify if a genuine recorded human voice has been replaced or swapped with another person’s AI-generated voice, by analyzing environmental inconsistencies and acoustic mismatches.

3. ## **Replay Detection of AI Voices**
   Detect when an AI-generated voice is played through a device (mobile, speaker) and then re-recorded, by capturing replay artifacts such as double reverberation, device frequency response, and background noise differences.
